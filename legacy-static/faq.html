Frequently Asked Questions

1️⃣ TREISA kya hai?
TREISA™ household cleaning aur personal hygiene products ka ek Indian brand hai. TREISA™ is a brand of Shrivandan Industries, jo ek proprietorship business hai aur products ka marketing aur distribution karta hai.
2️⃣ Kya TREISA khud products manufacture karta hai?
Nahi. TREISA™ ke sabhi products licensed third-party manufacturers ke dwara banaye jaate hain. In products ko TREISA™ brand ke under Shrivandan Industries (Proprietorship) market aur distribute karta hai.
3️⃣ TREISA ke kaun-kaun se products available hain?
TREISA™ brand ke under ye product categories available hain: Bath Soap, Dishwash Bar, Dishwash Liquid, Liquid Detergent, Hand Clean Liquid. Product availability location aur authorized distributor coverage par depend karti hai.
4️⃣ Kya main TREISA products website se online kharid sakta hoon?
Nahi. Is website par direct online purchase ya online payment facility available nahi hai. Products authorized distributors aur offline sales channels ke through supply kiye jaate hain.
5️⃣ Kya Cash on Delivery (COD) available hai?
Cash on Delivery (COD) select locations par available ho sakti hai. COD availability authorized distributor aur location par depend karti hai.
6️⃣ Main TREISA ka distributor kaise ban sakta hoon?
Aap website par available Distributor Enquiry Form ke through apni details submit kar sakte hain. Sabhi enquiries verification aur approval process ke baad hi consider ki jaati hain. Distributor appointment Shrivandan Industries ke discretion par hoti hai.
7️⃣ Distributor banne ke liye koi registration ya joining fee hoti hai?
Distributor terms, minimum order quantity aur commercial conditions location, business profile aur market potential ke basis par decide ki jaati hain. Final details approval ke baad hi share ki jaati hain.

Note: Ye FAQ general information ke liye hai. Final terms, conditions aur responsibilities website par available Terms & Conditions, Refund & Return Policy, Shipping & Distributor Supply Policy aur Disclaimer ke according govern hoti hain.
